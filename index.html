Deliciosa Comida Peruana

Delicious Peruvian Cuisine

Ceviche

A seafood dish that originated in Peru, typically made from fresh raw fish cured in citrus juices, such as lemon or lime, and spiced with ají, chili peppers or other seasonings including chopped onions, salt, and coriander.

Jalea

A cuisine dish consisting of fried fish, squid, and other seafood.

Lomo Saltado

A popular, traditional Peruvian dish, a stir fry that typically combines marinated strips of sirloin with onions, tomatoes, french fries, and other ingredients; and is typically served with rice.
